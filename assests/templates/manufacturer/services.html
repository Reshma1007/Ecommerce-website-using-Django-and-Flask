The Flipkart Assured badge is awarded to people who sign up for Flipkart Fulfilment and sell high quality products. To get this badge, a seller must fulfill the following conditions: Customer returns below a benchmark The listing should be Flipkart Fulfilment or Smart Fulfilment A product should enjoy a high rating above the benchmark. If you get the Flipkart Assured badge, then Flipkart applies the delivery charges. If you lose the Flipkart Assured badge, then you can set delivery charges based on your costs.
